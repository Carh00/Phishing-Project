Phishing

Phishing is known as a type of cyber criminal that uses various ways of communication, let it being email or social channels...

Why Its Bad

Whenever a successful phishing happens it causes damage to whoever was the victim. For example it can lead to financial loss, personal information risks, and also putting data and systems at risk.

How it Works?

Phishing works by directly trying to trick a user directly by messages or also by leading them to data harvesting sites or by also tricking the user to click on download links which can install malware onto the users device causing their information to be stolen.

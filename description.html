There are 4 different types of phishing which are known as “Spear Phishing”, “Whaling”, “Vishing”, and “Email Phishing”.

How to Tell

Requires just common sense and caution. Check out for poor grammar or punctuation, asking for personal information immediately, alarming content, or also just unrealistic rewards for providing personal information.
